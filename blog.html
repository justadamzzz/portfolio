Student Journey 9 June 2026

My Journey as a Computer Science Student

Studying Computer Science has taught me that learning technology is a long journey of curiosity, practice, and patience. Every assignment becomes a chance to improve how I think and solve problems.

I started by learning programming fundamentals, then moved into web development, database systems, and project-based work. The most important lesson is that good systems need both technical skill and clear planning.

Artificial Intelligence 9 June 2026

Why I Am Interested in Artificial Intelligence

Artificial intelligence interests me because it shows how software can learn from data, recognize patterns, and support better decision-making. It connects programming, mathematics, and real-world problem solving.

I want to understand how AI can be used responsibly in systems such as recommendation tools, chatbots, analytics dashboards, and automation that helps users work faster.

Web Projects 9 June 2026

Building Web Projects as a Software Development Student

Building web projects helps me understand how frontend design, backend logic, databases, and user experience work together. It also trains me to test, debug, and document my progress.

My favorite part is seeing an idea become interactive on the screen. Even a small feature, such as a search filter or form validation, can make a project feel more useful and complete.
